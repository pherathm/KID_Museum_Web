Home

This dashboard pulls together geographic engagement, trends over time, candidate school forecasting, and equity lenses to support KID Museum’s strategic decision-making.

How to use this dashboard
Use the left-hand navigation (similar to a Shiny sidebar) to move between views.
  • Geographic Engagement: Map of schools, programs, and equity buffers.
  • Trends & Attrition: Space for time-series charts and retention views.
  • Forecasting & Candidates: Future visuals for capacity and candidate schools.
  • Engagement Ladder: Planned Sankey or pathway visual for families and schools.
  • Equity & Methods: Definitions, filters, and notes on how “underserved” and “underrepresented” are defined.

Equity & Methods

Definitions and methodology for how “Title I”, “CEP”, high FARMs, “underserved”, and “underrepresented” are used in this dashboard.

Equity lenses used in the map
Brief notes (you can replace with your own wording).
  • Title I: Schools designated as Title I in 2025–26.
  • CEP: Schools participating in the Community Eligibility Provision.
  • High FARMs: Schools above a specified FARMs threshold (e.g., 40%).
  • Underserved / Underrepresented: Composite flags based on FARMs, race/ethnicity, and other indicators.
