This dashboard brings together geographic engagement, program trends, candidate schools, and equity lenses to support KID Museum’s strategic decision-making and partnerships.

How to use this dashboard
Use the left navigation to move between views (similar to a Shiny sidebar layout).
  • Geographic Engagement: Map of schools, program touchpoints, and equity buffers.
  • Trends & Attrition: Space for monthly/quarterly trends in program participation.
  • Forecasting & Candidates: Future visuals highlighting demand and candidate schools.
  • Engagement Ladder: Planned pathway/ladder visual for families and schools.
  • Equity & Methods: Definitions and methodological notes for equity categories.
